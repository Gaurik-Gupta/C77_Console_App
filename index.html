Setting Up Visual Studio

To use Visual Studio Code, you will need to download it. Once downloaded, you are ready to code in almost any language you can think of!

Using Your Console

There are two ways to open your console. The first way is to right-click and the click on Inspect. By default, you will be on the Elements tab. Click on the Console tab to see your console. The second and shorter way is to use the Ctrl + Shift + J shortcut. To print anything on the console with JavaScript, just use the "console.log('whatever you want to print')" and it will be printed in the console
